#DHBenelux tweep interaction

By Ben Verhoeven & Florian Kunneman

This visualization is our result of the Antwerp Spring Aca­demy 2015 in Digi­tal Humani­ties on data visualization. We decided to visualize the interactions on Twitter in relation to the DHBenelux conference, and compare the edition in The Hague in 2014 and the edition in Antwerp in 2015.

We collected all tweets that refer to '#DHBenelux', and were posted during either of the two conferences (not before or after them). This resulted in about 1000 tweets per edition. We removed the retweets from these and made counts of the tweets per username. To obtain a sense of the interaction, we distinguished tweets that contains a user mention (e.g. @verhoevenben or @fkunneman) from tweets that do not. The tweet frequencies per user account are displayed by the height of their bar. The unique number of users that are interacted with are indicated by the color intensity. The graph enables you to play around with the ordering of the data. Enjoy!
