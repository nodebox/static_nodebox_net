Description

This is a unique calendar. Each season has a different color, each month has a different shape which depends on when particular month starts and ends, each week has a line, so you don't need any day numbers. There is a weekends in the red columns. Orange stars shows you celebrations and other Lithuania national holidays. And now.... it is your time to mark some important days for you! Choose year,month, day and type of your event and you will see how your own calendar looks like. And only you will be able to read it. Make it interesting, different and unique.
